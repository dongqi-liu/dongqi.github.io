I am currently a 2-nd year M.Sc. student in the Department of Computer Science at University of Amsterdam and Vrije University Amsterdam . My research interests lie primarily in the area of AI X Linguistics, such as Natural Language Processing, Understanding and Generation.

Currently, I am completing my master thesis under the supervision of Prof. Khalil Sima'an at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation, University of Amsterdam. The research topic of my master thesis is how to incorporate dependency forest structure to help the (self-) attention mechanism in the Transformer-based model to better pay attention to the syntax relationship between the tokens of the input sentences in the Neural Machine Translation (NMT) tasks.
